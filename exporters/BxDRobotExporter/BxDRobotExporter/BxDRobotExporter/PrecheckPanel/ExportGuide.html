Robot Export Guide

Learn how to restructure assemblies within Inventor so that moving parts are accessible to the Synthesis Robot Exporter plugin.

  1. Restructure moving parts in your assembly.
  2. At the top of the Inventor window, add joints to your assemblies. Under the Assemble tab, click on the Joint button.
  3. Under the Environments tab, click on the Robot Export ribbon. Assign your drive train using the Drive Train Type button.
  4. Use the Drive Train Weight button to set your drivetrain weight.
  5. Configure the details of your joints using the Edit Joints button.
    1. Joint setup
    2. Limits setup
    3. Sensor setup
  6. Configure/setup the degrees of freedom using the Degrees of Freedom button.
