Robot Export Guide

  • Separate moving parts of your design into separate components at the top level of your assembly tree
  • Use the Joint button under the Assemble tab at the top of the Inventor window to properly joint your assemblies
  • Assign your drive train type using the Drive Train Type button in the Robot Export ribbon
  • Set your drivetrain weight using the Drive Train Weight button in the Robot Export ribbon
  • Set up the details of your joints using the Edit Joints button in the Robot Export ribbon
    • Joint setup
    • Joint limits settings
    • Sensor setup
  • Set up the degrees of freedom of your joints using the Degrees of Freedom button in the Robot Export ribbon
