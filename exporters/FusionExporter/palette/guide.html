Robot Export Guide

  1. Separate moving parts of your design into separate components at the top level of your assembly tree
  2. Use the Joint button under the Assemble tab at the top of the Inventor window to add joints to your assemblies
  3. Assign your drive train type using the Drive Train Type button under the Robot Export ribbon at the top of the window
  4. Set your drivetrain weight using the Drive Train Weight button under the Robot Export ribbon at the top of the window
  5. Set up the details of your joints using the Edit Joints button under the Robot Export ribbon at the top of the window
  6. Learn how to restructure assemblies within Inventor so that moving parts are accessible to the Synthesis Robot Exporter plugin.
  7. At the top of the Inventor window, add joints to your assemblies. Under the Assemble tab, click on the Joint button.
  8. Under the Environments tab, click on the Robot Export ribbon. Assign your drive train using the Drive Train Type button.
  9. Use the Drive Train Weight button to set your drivetrain weight.
  10. Configure the details of your joints using the Edit Joints button.
    1. Joint setup
    2. Limits setup
    3. Sensor setup
  11. Set up the degrees of freedom of your joints using the Degrees of Freedom button under the Robot Export ribbon at the top of the window
  12. Configure/setup the degrees of freedom using the Degrees of Freedom button.
