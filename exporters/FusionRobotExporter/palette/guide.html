Robot Export Guide

  1. Separate moving parts of your design into separate components at the top level of your assembly tree (YouTube Tutorial: https://youtu.be/fY3Vdkh8L0Y)
  2. At the top of the Fusion 360 window, add joints to your assemblies. Under the Assemble tab, click the Joint button. (YouTube Tutorial: https://youtu.be/_YCP-cWn1Gc)
  3. Assign your drive train type using the Drive Train Type button under the Drive Train Setup category.
  4. Set your drive train weight using the Drive Train Weight under the Drive Train Setup category.
  5. Configure the details of your joints using the Edit Joints button under the Joint Setup category
  6. View the degrees of freedom using the Toggle Degrees of Freedom View button under the Robot Setup Checklist category.
